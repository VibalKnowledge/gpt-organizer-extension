How to Get Started

  1. Navigate to chat.openai.com or chatgpt.com
  2. Click the GPT Organizer extension icon in your browser toolbar
  3. Create your first folder by clicking the "+ Folder" button
  4. Add GPTs to your folders by clicking "+ Add GPT to folder"
  5. Use the search box to quickly find specific GPTs
  6. Click on any GPT name to open it directly in ChatGPT
