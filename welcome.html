How to Get Started

  1. Navigate to chat.openai.com and open any custom GPT
  2. You'll see the GPT Organizer appear in the sidebar with your current GPT highlighted at the top
  3. Click the "+ Create Folder" button to make your first folder
  4. Right-click on your current GPT (or any GPT in Recent GPTs) to add it to a folder
  5. Use the search box at the bottom to find specific GPTs you've organized
  6. Click the gear icon (‚öô) in the header to access settings and keyboard shortcuts
